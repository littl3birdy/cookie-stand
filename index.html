Pat's Salmon Cookies

It all started with a craving for some good old Wild Alaskan Salmon and some cookies. The rest is history.

Fishy Goodness for Everyone!

Pat has been making these delicious confections for his family for the last 5 years, and has finally decided to share this fishy goodness with the rest of the world!

Each of our handcrafted cookies starts with Wild Alaskan salmon caught at the peak of the season in Bristol Bay. Pat has a contract with a privately owned fishing company that uses sustainable fishing techniques so you can feel good about your fishy cookies. Flour, sugar, butter, salmon, frosting and a whole lotta love make for a delectable treat that can't be beat!
